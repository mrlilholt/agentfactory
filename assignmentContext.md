Systems Studio: AI Agent Lab! Build Your Own Digital Product with AI Superstars! 

Forget working alone! In this project, you're the Human Director of a digital studio, leading a team of super-smart AI Agents.
Your Mission:
Decide what cool product you'll build.
Guide your AI agents with clear instructions.
Assign specific tasks to each agent.
Review and check their work (you're the boss!).
Manage the project until it's finished and ready to show off.
